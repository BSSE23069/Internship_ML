About This Project

Built with care & science

Every design decision was made with emotional safety in mind.

🧠
Fine-Tuned LLM

DistilGPT2 was fine-tuned on Facebook AI's EmpatheticDialogues — 25,000+ real human conversations grounded in emotional situations across 32 emotion categories.

💬
Emotion-Conditioned

Each response is conditioned on your selected emotional state using special tokens. This guides the model to respond with appropriate warmth and empathy.

🛡️
Safety First

Serenity does not provide medical diagnoses or clinical advice. It is designed to offer compassionate companionship and always refers users to professional resources for serious concerns.
